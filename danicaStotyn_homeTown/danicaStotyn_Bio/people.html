The people of Tokyo

The Heart of Tokyo

The city of Tokyo has an estimated population of 37,036,200 as of 2025, with the population having a slight decline from past years. With over 65% of the population being 65+ years of age, withpeople of the ages 18-64 being only 21.5% of the population. The area is a popular place for those of retirement age with its wide area of scenery it is a perfect place for any type of person. Tokyo Japan has a very small foreigner population, with foreigners only making up less than 5% of the total population as of 2020. With the rest of the population being those born and raised in Japan. Though foreigners are not the most common they are always welcomed.
