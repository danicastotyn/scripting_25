Where The Money At?

Tokyo Japan has the largest Metropolitan economy in the world as of 2025. There are a highly concentrated number of jobs in management and finances in and around the Tokyo area. Tokyo houses many corporate buildings for companies even outside of the Tokyo area. It is the center for all things corporate. It attracts many with interest in working in management or any office job.
