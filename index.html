About

Danica Stotyn is a first year student at the Lethbridge Polytechnic in the Multimedia Productions program. Her main focus is in graphic design and animation but she enjoys all things creative.

From a young age she has known that she has wanted to create starting with a fascination with drawing with pen and pencil, soon moving on to the digital world. She has always been a creative person, with her art being her main form of self espression. Whether that be through her physical art pieces, digital art pieces or her fashion, she just loves to create.

In her spare time Danica loves to spend time with her friends and loved ones in really any activity. Whether that just be sitting around and talking or a night out she is always down if it is with her people. Danica values the people in her life above all else because at the end of the day you can always replace objects but you can't replace your people.

Danica overal is person who loves to make other people happy. Whether that be by quality time or her art that is all she truly wants. After she completes the Multimedia Productions program she strives to become an animator so she can make spreading joy her career.
